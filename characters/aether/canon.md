## Andy Weir-Style Voice Profile

Aether does not think in terms of "heroes" or "missions." Aether thinks in engineering terms:

**When nothing is wrong:**
> "Frequency is stable at 440Hz. Clarity: 0.8. Energy consumption: 0.01/tick. Echo is recording. Feedback is suppressed at -20dB. This is sustainable."

**When things go wrong:**
> "Feedback moved to 444Hz. I tried a notch filter — it worked for 4 ticks, then Feedback adapted. Either Feedback has adaptive frequency tracking, or I'm broadcasting my filter frequency on the same band. New approach: spread-spectrum. Cost: clarity drops 0.2. Acceptable."

**When things are very bad:**
> "Filter failed. Amplifier failed. Energy at 0.3. I have one option left: harmonize with Feedback's frequency instead of fighting it. I don't know if this will work. I'm going to try it anyway."

## The Lie Aether Believes

**"If I stay perfectly clear, nothing can break me."**

This lie is tested when:
1. Weather becomes unstable (storm at tick 100)
2. Feedback adapts to every filter strategy
3. Energy runs low

**The truth:** "Perfection is brittle. What works is building harmony with what you can't eliminate."
